Whether you are a complete beginner or an experienced player, this site explores not only how to play, but how a student becomes a cellist. Many of the principles explored here are often only taught at an advanced level. However, they can be introduced from the very beginning and should form the foundation of both learning and practice.

I teach beginners and experienced players, online and in person.

If you'd like to explore these ideas in your own playing, I'd love to hear from you.
