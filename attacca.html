Three Latin Dances for Cello (& piano)
A fun Latin suite of three contrasting pieces for intermediate cellists (Grades 3–5).

Pass the Salsa
Approximately Grade 3. A fiery introduction to Latin rhythm, developing confidence with focus on backward extensions.

Cello Go Bolero
Approximately Grade 4. An energetic concert piece and ideal introduction to tenor clef.

Tangissimo
Approximately Grade 5. A passionate tango shifting through the first 4 positions.
